Process

How the signals are generated

01 5:00 PM ET daily
Data Refresh

25 tickers fetched from yfinance. Technical features, point-in-time fundamentals, VIX, and sector momentum updated.

02 6:00 AM ET
Regime Check

Live VIX + SPY 200-DMA check. If VIX > 28 or SPY is below its 200-DMA, all signals are blocked regardless of model output.

03 6:10 AM ET
Model Scoring

GBM classifier scores all 25 tickers. Only signals where model confidence ≥ 60% proceed. Average: 1–3 per day.

04 6:15 AM ET
Telegram Alert

Top signals delivered with ticker, direction, confidence level, and the 3-factor reasoning chain. Before any exchange opens.

05 4:15 PM ET
Outcome Card

Public outcome card posted to X showing entry price, EOD close, and the actual return. Full transparency, every day.

Infrastructure

Major firm ML standards, applied here

🔬

Point-in-time fundamentals

PE ratios and FCF yields joined via ASOF merge on quarterly snapshots — never using today's values for historical training rows.

🛡

Purged walk-forward CV

A 5-day purge gap is applied at each fold boundary to prevent target-window overlap between training and test data.

📊

188 KPIs

Technical indicators, volatility metrics, sector momentum, insider flow, earnings proximity, and macro factors — all computed per ticker.

🌊

VIX regime filter

CLEAR (VIX ≤ 20), WARNING (VIX 20–28), BLOCKED (VIX > 28). Position sizing scales automatically with regime state.

⚡

Confidence threshold

Signals only fire when model confidence ≥ 60%. The model is selective by design — some days zero signals is the right answer.

🔄

Weekly retraining

Model retrains every Sunday. The most recent market regime is always represented. No stale weights.
