Process

How the signals are generated

From data refresh to Telegram alert in under 15 minutes. Every weekday.

5:00 PM ET
Data Refresh

25 tickers updated. Technical features, point-in-time fundamentals, VIX, and sector momentum computed for all.

Infrastructure

Major-firm ML standards, applied here

The same methodological discipline used at quantitative shops — deployed on Google Cloud.

Point-in-time fundamentals

PE ratios and FCF yields joined via ASOF merge on quarterly snapshots. No lookahead bias — today's values never touch historical training rows.

Purged walk-forward CV

5-day purge gap at every fold boundary. Target-window overlap between training and validation is impossible by design.

188 KPIs

Technical indicators, volatility, sector momentum, insider flow, earnings proximity, EPS revisions, macro factors — all per ticker.

VIX regime filter

CLEAR (≤ 20) · WARNING (20–28, reduced sizing) · BLOCKED (> 28). Position sizing scales with regime automatically.

Confidence threshold

Signals only fire at ≥ 60% model confidence. Some days zero signals is the right answer — and that's what you get.

Weekly retraining

Model retrains every Sunday on the full available history. The most recent market regime is always represented in the weights.
